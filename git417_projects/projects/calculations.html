Project 1: Calculations

For this week's project, I've created a simple budget planner. By inputting your monthly income and allocating how much money you wish (or need) to spend in each category, the planner then calculates how much money is left over so that you can change your budget costs as necessary.

The goal of this project is to get users to start being more conscious about how they should spend their monthly income in a relaxed and interactive setting that lets them adjust how much money they want to spend in each category until they're happy with the results. It's also a great way for users to test how much money they could spend for living and miscellaneous expenses by inputting a hypothetical income that they hope to one day earn.
