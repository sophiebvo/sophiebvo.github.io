Project 2: If/Else Statements

For this week's project, I've updated my simple budget planner from the previous week to provide more intuitive responses after a user calculates their remaining money. Just as before, users input their monthly income and allocate how much money they wish (or need) to spend in each category, and the planner then calculates how much money is left over so that they can change their budget costs as necessary. The planner now also provides suggestions to the user based on how much money is left over, and the amount of money left over changes color depending on how little or how much money is left.

If you would like to compare the response changes side by side, last week's project is available in the Calculations page.
