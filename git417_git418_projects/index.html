Project 7: Custom Object

In this project, users can make a custom workout plan by creating and adding exercises to a table, adding as many as they wish. Users can choose to count their exercises either in repetitions or in minutes, as well as how many repetitions or how many minutes they wish to spend on the exercise. They cannot add any exercise without completing filling out the form, and if they mess up by adding an exercise they hadn't intended, they can clear the last exercise by clicking on a button under the table.
